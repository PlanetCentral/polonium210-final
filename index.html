Introduction to Polonium-210

Polonium-210 (Po-210) is a highly radioactive element, occurs through decay of radon gas and uranium. It poses significant risks due to its extreme toxicity, even in microscopic amounts.

This element gained infamy for its use in high-profile poisonings but is also found in trace amounts in tobacco and industrial sectors, making it a critical concern for public health.
